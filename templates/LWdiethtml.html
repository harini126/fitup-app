Weight Loss Diet

Breakfast

Wheat, Carbohydrates- Roti, Idli, Dosa, Poha, etc
Fruits - Banana, Orange, apple, melon, grapes Oats , Milk

Lunch

Wholegrains - Rice, Millets, Barley Fiber - Carrot, Peas, Leafy vegetables, etc. Other vegetables in a sufficient quantity

Dinner

Roti, Parotta, Brown rice With vegetable curry in a sufficient quantity (better to take a bit more than regular) Fruits or oats milk

Added tips

Drink water: Stay hydrated by drinking water throughout the day. Sometimes, thirst is mistaken for hunger.
Don't skip meals: Eat regular, balanced meals to avoid overeating later in the day.
Stay active: Include regular exercise in your routine, aiming for a mix of cardio and strength training.
