Added tips

Portion Control: Pay attention to portion sizes. Even healthy foods can lead to weight gain if consumed in excessive amounts.
Balance between the calories you consume and the calories you burn through daily activities and exercise.
Hydration: Stay well-hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
