Muscle Gain Workouts

Knee Push-Ups

Knee Push-ups strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps and are a versatile bodyweight exercise for muscle development.

Incline Push-Ups

Incline Push-ups strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps and are a versatile bodyweight exercise for muscle development.

Tricep Dips

Dips primarily work the chest and triceps. They can be performed using parallel bars or with a bench.

Side Plank

Planks and other core exercises help strengthen the abdominal and core muscles, providing stability and supporting overall strength.

Russian Twists

Russian twists work the obliques and help strengthen the core and side abdominal muscles.

Squats

Squats target the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, lower back, and core. They are considered one of the most effective exercises for building overall lower body strength and muscle mass.

Lunges

Lunges engage the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, helping to build leg strength and muscle.

Bulgarian Split Squats

Bulgarian split squats are an excellent exercise for targeting and building the muscles of the lower body, particularly the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. They provide an opportunity for progressive overload by increasing the weight over time.

Bench Press

The bench press targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It's a fundamental exercise for building a strong and well-developed chest.

Crunches

While crunches have their place in core training and can help you maintain strong and defined abdominal muscles, it's essential to incorporate a well-rounded strength training routine that includes compound exercises for the entire body if your goal is to gain muscle mass.
