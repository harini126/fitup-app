Weight Gain Workouts

Tricep dips

Another easy arm and back exercise you can perform at home to gain weight is triceps dips. If performed correctly, dips might aid in upper body muscle growth.

Burpees

Burpees are a high-intensity, full-body exercise that combines strength training, cardiovascular conditioning, and explosiveness, making them effective for improving overall fitness and burning calories.

Pull-Ups

Pull-up is an effective exercise for gaining weight. This exercise works well for developing chiselled shoulders and ripped arms.

Squats

Squats are one of the most fundamental exercises for the lower body that can be used to strengthen and tone it, and they are frequently incorporated into weight-gain workouts.

Bench Press

The bench press is a great exercise for developing your chest muscles. However, it's important to carry out this weight-gain routine correctly, just like with any other exercise.

Crunches

Muscle development must be one of your workout objectives. Crunches are a great way to build muscle and strengthen your core.

Lunges

Like squats, lunges help build and tone your hip and leg muscles. It's one of the best methods for gaining weight.

Push-Ups

Push-ups are renowned for strengthening the upper body and core. Although a little challenging, these are very effective for weight gain.
