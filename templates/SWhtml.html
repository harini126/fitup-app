Stable Weight Workouts

Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks can be one part of your overall fitness program, along with other activities like strength training, flexibility exercises, and a balanced diet. Consistency in your exercise routine and managing your calorie intake are key factors in weight maintenance.

Leg rises

Leg raises are a bodyweight exercise that can contribute to your fitness routine and help maintain a constant weight. They primarily target the muscles in your lower abdomen and hip flexors.

Burpees

Burpees are a high-intensity, full-body exercise that can be effective for maintaining a constant weight when incorporated into a well-rounded fitness routine.

Mountain Climbing

This exercise is a form of high-intensity cardiovascular exercise that engages various muscle groups. Here's how mountain climbers can contribute to weight maintenance.

Squats

Squats are an excellent exercise for maintaining a constant weight and overall fitness. They are a compound movement that primarily targets the lower body, but they also engage your core and help build muscle throughout your body

Plank

Planks are an isometric core exercise that strengthens your abdominal muscles, lower back, and stabilizes your entire core, helping improve posture, reduce the risk of back pain, and enhance overall core strength and endurance. Also reduces the abdominal fat.

Lunges

Lunges are a great bodyweight exercise that can be included in your fitness routine to help maintain a constant weight. Lunges target the muscles in your lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves

Push-Ups

Push-ups can be an effective exercise for weight maintenance when incorporated into your fitness routine as part of a balanced approach. While push-ups primarily target the muscles in the upper body and triceps, they offer several benefits that support weight management
