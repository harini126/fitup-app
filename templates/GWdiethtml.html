Added tips

Drink water: Stay hydrated by drinking water throughout the day. Sometimes, thirst is mistaken for hunger.
Don't skip meals: Eat regular, balanced meals to avoid overeating later in the day.
Empty Calories: Avoid foods and drinks with empty calories, such as sugary sweets and carbonated beverages.
